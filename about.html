About Oomf St.

Oomf St. (pronounced oomph street) is a project spatially visualising my network of online friends; we might live hundreds and thousands of miles away from each other, but we are still neighbours! While the internet is not a "true" physical brick-and-mortar place, it's the memories and experiences that make a place a place.

OOMF stands for One Of My Followers, but can also stand for One Of My Friends. This website is dedicated to the online friends I've made throughout the past six years

This was done as part of experimentation for visual communication design honours.
